About Us

Welcome to Muffin-hut, your number one source for all your pastry
and kenyan cuisine We're dedicated to giving you the very best
of pastries with a focus on customer service and uniqueness.
It was founded in 2018.

Our mission is that in every cent we get as profit
we use it to create safe houses for children
in unsafe house or the street children.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering
them to you. If you have any questions or comments,
please don't hesitate to contact us.
